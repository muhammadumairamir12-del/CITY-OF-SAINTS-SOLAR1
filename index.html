FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?+
Absolutely not! At Al-Fazal, root canal treatment is completely pain-free thanks to our advanced local anesthesia techniques. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the procedure is.
How long does teeth whitening last?+
Professional teeth whitening results typically last between 1 to 3 years. We recommend touch-up sessions every 6-12 months depending on your lifestyle.
What is the cost of dental implants?+
Please consult with our team for implant pricing. We offer flexible installment payment options. Dental implants can last 15-25 years or longer with proper care.
What age is best for braces?+
Braces are highly effective for children aged 10-14, but equally effective for adults. We also offer clear aligners as a discreet option for adult orthodontics.
Do you treat dental emergencies?+
Yes! Al-Fazal Hospital is open daily until 11:30 PM and we welcome all dental emergencies. Call us immediately at 0321-4105096 for urgent care.
How do I book an appointment?+
You can call us at 0321-4105096, WhatsApp us, or click "Book Now" on our website. Walk-ins are also always welcome. First consultation is completely FREE!
